Streamlining GIS Processes for Cambium Inc.​

Simplifying the air photo selection process by creating a web application and streamlining the extraction and delivery of well water data.​

Our client, Cambium Inc., requested two solutions as part of the project:

  • Streamline their internal Well Water Information System, and
  • Modify the processing and retrieval of aerial imagery (including internal access without enterprise-level software licensing).

To achieve these objectives, we divided the project into two deliverables.

The first replaced Cambium's existing well water database with an ArcGIS Pro server connection to the Ministry of the Environment, Conservation and Parks publicly hosted well water feature layer.

For the second deliverable we developed a web application using Leaflet; to support the web application we also developed a process to convert images to Cloud Optimized GeoTIFF, and created a GeoJSON data store to support the retrieval of images and their associated data.

Consideration was given to ongoing maintenance and costs with the use of open-source software and pre-existing software licensing. Optimizing storage and retrieval solutions for both well water data and aerial imagery were also key considerations.
